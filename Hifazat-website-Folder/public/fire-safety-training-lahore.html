Fire Safety Training in Lahore

HIFAZAT Fire Safety Solutions · Updated July 2026 · Lahore, Pakistan

An extinguisher on the wall doesn't help if nobody knows how to use it. HIFAZAT delivers on-site fire safety training for staff and building occupants across Lahore — practical, hands-on, using your building's own layout and equipment, not a generic slideshow.

What's covered

  • Extinguisher operation — hands-on practice, not just verbal instruction
  • Fire type recognition — which extinguisher suits which fire, and why using the wrong one can make things worse
  • Evacuation awareness — exit routes specific to your building, assembly points, headcount procedure
  • First-minute response — what to do the moment a fire is discovered, before help arrives

Why on-site training works better

Generic fire safety training teaches generic buildings. HIFAZAT trains your staff using your actual building — the exit they'd really use, the extinguisher that's really on their floor, the assembly point that's really outside your gate. That specificity is what makes training stick when it's actually needed.

How a session works

  1. Scheduling. We coordinate a time that works for your team, factoring in shift patterns for factories or retail staff.
  2. Walkthrough. Staff are shown their building's actual exits, alarm points and extinguisher locations.
  3. Hands-on practice. Extinguisher operation is practiced, not just described.
  4. Q&A. Staff can ask about specific scenarios relevant to their work area.

Who benefits from this

Offices, factories, schools, restaurants and commercial plazas all have staff who are the actual first responders in the minutes before Rescue 1122 arrives. Training pairs naturally with a fire risk assessment — once your equipment gap is closed, training makes sure the people in the building can actually use what's there.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does fire safety training cover?

HIFAZAT's training covers how to correctly use a fire extinguisher, recognizing different fire types and which extinguisher suits each, evacuation route awareness, and what to do in the first minute of discovering a fire — before the fire brigade arrives.

Is training done on-site at our building?

Yes. HIFAZAT conducts training on-site using your building's actual equipment and evacuation routes, which is more useful than generic classroom training since staff learn the specific layout they'd need to navigate in an emergency.

How many staff can attend a training session?

Session size depends on your building and team — HIFAZAT can accommodate small office teams or larger factory and plaza staff groups. Contact us with your team size to plan the session.

Does training include a live extinguisher demonstration?

Yes, hands-on extinguisher practice is part of the training where practical, so staff aren't just told how to use one — they've actually held and operated one before they need to in a real situation.
