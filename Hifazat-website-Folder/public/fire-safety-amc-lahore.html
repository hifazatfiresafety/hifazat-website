Fire Safety AMC in Lahore

HIFAZAT Fire Safety Solutions · Updated July 2026 · Lahore, Pakistan

Installing fire safety equipment is the easy part. Keeping it working — refilled, tested, functional — is where most buildings quietly fall behind. HIFAZAT's Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) puts extinguisher refilling, alarm testing and system checks on a schedule, so nothing expires silently between inspections.

What's covered under an AMC

  • Extinguisher checks & refilling — pressure verified, refilled before expiry, not after
  • Fire alarm testing — panel function, detector response, sounder audibility
  • Hydrant & sprinkler checks — where installed, pump function and pressure verified
  • Maintenance records — a documented history of every visit, useful for your own compliance records

How it works

  1. Initial assessment. We review your building's existing equipment, whether installed by HIFAZAT or another provider.
  2. Schedule agreed. Visit frequency is set based on your building's equipment and risk profile — typically quarterly or bi-annual.
  3. Scheduled visits. Each visit checks and services equipment per the agreed scope, with findings documented.
  4. Ongoing coverage. Your building stays inspection-ready without you tracking individual expiry dates yourself.

Why buildings need this, not just a one-time install

Equipment doesn't fail loudly — extinguisher pressure drops gradually, detector sensitivity degrades with dust, and panels can develop faults that go unnoticed until tested. An AMC catches these before an inspector or, worse, an actual fire does. It's the difference between having fire safety equipment and having fire safety equipment that works.

Who this is for

Any building that's already invested in fire safety equipment — alarm systems, extinguishers, hydrant systems or sprinklers — benefits from an AMC. This is especially important for commercial plazas, factories, schools and hospitals where equipment failure has higher consequences.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does a fire safety AMC include?

HIFAZAT's AMC includes scheduled extinguisher checks and refilling, fire alarm panel and detector testing, hydrant and sprinkler system checks where installed, and a maintenance record for each visit — so your building's equipment is verified working, not just installed once and forgotten.

How often does HIFAZAT visit under an AMC?

Visit frequency depends on your building's equipment and risk profile — typically quarterly or bi-annual checks with an annual comprehensive service. The exact schedule is agreed when the contract is set up.

Is an AMC only for buildings that already have equipment installed?

No. HIFAZAT can start an AMC alongside a new installation, or take over maintenance for equipment already installed by another provider, following an initial assessment to confirm its condition.

What happens if equipment fails during an AMC period?

Faults found during scheduled visits are flagged and addressed as part of the contract terms. Contact HIFAZAT directly to confirm what's covered under repair versus replacement for your specific agreement.
