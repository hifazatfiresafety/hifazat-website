FAQs

Fire Safety — Common Questions

Answers to questions building owners in Lahore ask most often.

Which fire safety services does HIFAZAT provide in Lahore?

HIFAZAT provides fire alarm system installation, fire extinguisher supply and refilling, fire hydrant and sprinkler system installation, fire risk assessment, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for commercial plazas, factories, schools, hospitals, restaurants, offices and residential buildings across Lahore.

Do you provide Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes. HIFAZAT offers AMC contracts that include scheduled inspections, fire extinguisher refilling, alarm system testing and equipment servicing throughout the year, keeping your building compliant and your systems ready.

Do you refill fire extinguishers?

Yes. HIFAZAT refills and replaces CO2 and DCP fire extinguishers for all building types. We check the extinguisher's condition, refill it to the correct pressure, and ensure it is inspection-ready.

Which areas of Lahore do you serve?

HIFAZAT serves all areas of Lahore. Our office is located at 3B Nespak Phase-3, opposite DHA Rahbar Gate-5, Defence Road, and our team travels across the city for site inspections and installations.

How do I know how many fire extinguishers or detectors my building needs?

The number depends on your building's floor area, number of floors, and usage type (commercial, residential or mixed). HIFAZAT conducts a site inspection to assess your building and recommends the correct equipment based on fire safety requirements.

What types of fire alarm systems do you install?

HIFAZAT installs conventional zone-based fire alarm systems, including the control panel, smoke detectors, heat detectors, manual call points (MCPs) and sounders, designed floor-by-floor for your building.

How can I request a fire safety inspection?

You can request a fire safety inspection by calling or messaging HIFAZAT on 0309-1666636, or by filling the inquiry form on hifazatfiresafety.com. The team reviews your building details and arranges a site visit.

What kinds of buildings do you work with?

HIFAZAT works with commercial plazas, factories and warehouses, schools and academies, hospitals, restaurants and cafes, offices, and residential buildings — providing fire safety solutions suited to each building type.
