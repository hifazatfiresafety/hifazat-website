What we supply

  • DCP extinguishers — the all-purpose choice for shops, offices and corridors

How supply and delivery works

  1. Tell us your building type. Shop, office, factory floor, or home — the right extinguisher type and count depends on this.
  2. We recommend quantity and placement. For single units this is quick; for plazas and multi-floor buildings we do a short site assessment.
  3. Delivery and mounting. Extinguishers are delivered and wall-mounted at the correct height and location — near exits and risk points, visible, not tucked in a store room.
  4. Documentation. You get what you need for inspection readiness.

Bulk supply for plazas and buildings

Commercial plazas and multi-tenant buildings often need extinguishers across several floors, each with different risk profiles — a kitchen floor needs different coverage than a storage floor. HIFAZAT handles this as one coordinated order rather than treating each floor separately, so placement is consistent across the whole building. This pairs well with our fire alarm installation service when a building needs both.

After supply — refilling and AMC

Extinguishers we supply don't end at delivery. HIFAZAT provides refilling when pressure drops, and our Annual Maintenance Contract keeps your building's extinguishers checked and refilled on a schedule, so nothing expires silently between inspections.
