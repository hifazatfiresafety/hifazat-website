Fire Extinguisher Refilling in Lahore

HIFAZAT Fire Safety Solutions · Updated July 2026 · Lahore, Pakistan

An extinguisher that hasn't been refilled is the same as not having one — it just looks like you do. HIFAZAT refills DCP and CO2 extinguishers across Lahore, checking pressure, condition and seals so your equipment actually works when it's needed, not just when it's inspected.

Signs your extinguisher needs refilling

  • Gauge needle outside the green zone — the most direct sign of low pressure
  • Used, even partially — any discharge means it needs an immediate refill
  • Past its annual service date — refill regardless of gauge reading
  • Visible corrosion, dents or damaged hose — may need replacement, not just refilling

How our refilling service works

  1. Assessment. We check each extinguisher's pressure, condition and expiry.
  2. Refill or replace. Working units are refilled to correct pressure; damaged units are flagged for replacement.
  3. Re-mounting. Extinguishers are returned to their correct wall-mounted position.
  4. Record. You get a record of what was serviced and when — useful for your own inspection readiness.

For buildings with multiple extinguishers, we can refill on-site to avoid gaps in coverage. For single units, collection and return is usually simpler.

Why refilling gets forgotten — and why it matters

Extinguishers sit quietly on walls for years, which is exactly why refilling schedules get missed — nothing visibly changes until pressure has already dropped. During an actual Rescue 1122 or building inspection, an expired extinguisher counts as a missing one. Refilling is the cheapest part of fire safety compliance, and the easiest to overlook.

Skip the reminder — put it on a schedule

If your building has more than a few extinguishers, tracking individual refill dates by memory doesn't scale. HIFAZAT's Annual Maintenance Contract puts refilling on autopilot — we track expiry and refill before it becomes a compliance gap.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my fire extinguisher needs refilling?

Check the pressure gauge — if the needle is outside the green zone, it needs refilling. Extinguishers should also be refilled after any use, even partial, and checked annually regardless of the gauge reading, since seals and powder can degrade over time.

Does HIFAZAT collect extinguishers for refilling or refill on-site?

Both, depending on your building. For single units we can often collect and return, and for buildings with multiple extinguishers we arrange on-site refilling to minimise the time your building is without coverage.

How often should fire extinguishers be refilled in Lahore?

Annually at minimum, even if unused, as part of standard maintenance. Extinguishers used during an actual fire or test must be refilled immediately regardless of schedule.

Can refilling be included in an Annual Maintenance Contract?

Yes. HIFAZAT's AMC includes scheduled refilling as part of the contract, so extinguishers across your building are refilled automatically before they expire, without you needing to track individual dates.
